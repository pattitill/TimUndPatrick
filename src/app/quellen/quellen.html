Sources

Below are the sources we used for data, images, and inspiration for our game:

  • Freedom House - Freedom in the World Scores

    This website provides freedom scores for various countries, measuring political rights and civil liberties.

  • Wikipedia - World Press Freedom Index

    Our primary source for press freedom rankings, compiled annually by Reporters Without Borders.

  • Microsoft Designer - AI Tool

    We used this tool to help generate the background images for the game.

  • Imgur - Image Hosting

    The backgrounds for our game are hosted on Imgur, a free image hosting platform.

  • The Higher Lower Game

    This game served as inspiration for the mechanics of our own "Higher or Lower" freedom of speech game.
